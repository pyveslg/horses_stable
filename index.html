Get Horses Stable Manager

Horses Stable Manager is a powerful and simple task manager for your horses stable, right in your pocket.

It helps professional horses stable teams and private horse owners plan and monitor all their daily activities with their horses.

Designed by professionals

Horses Stable Manager was designed and proof-tested by real professionals in France and is now trusted by dozens of stable managers.

Thanks to their feedbacks, we have been iterating for a year to deliver a truly useful and adapted application to stable teams to help them monitor their daily activies with horses.

Manage your horses and activities

With Horses Stable Manager, you can easily set and organize any custom activities and tasks that should be performed for each of your horses and assign them to any members of your staff. The app adapts to your own stable organization for a seamless integration into your daily workflow.
